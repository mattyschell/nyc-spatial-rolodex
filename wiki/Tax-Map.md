The Department of Finance maintains the [Property Information Portal](https://propertyinformationportal.nyc.gov/) (PIP, pronounced "pipp").  The Department of Finance publishes the spatial components of the Property Information Portal as services on the [NYCMaps](https://nyc.maps.arcgis.com/home/index.html) ArcGIS Online organization.  They have grouped these services under several items.

## Digital Tax Map Daily View

The data under this item should reflect the current data displayed on the property information portal. Use caution with these services, they are intended internal Department of Finance use and may change. 

https://nyc.maps.arcgis.com/home/item.html?id=2cf51c5f165c4c569691617a492d9b21

## Digital Tax Map (Monthly) View

The data under this item should be more stable. It is intended for consumers/publishers like NYC Open Data.

https://nyc.maps.arcgis.com/home/item.html?id=8bf9967980764e95bc6c2b6f6abbcbdd

Add #data to preview the tabular data.

https://nyc.maps.arcgis.com/home/item.html?id=8bf9967980764e95bc6c2b6f6abbcbdd#data
