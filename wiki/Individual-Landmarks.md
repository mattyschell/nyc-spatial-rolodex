# Open Data Portal

https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Housing-Development/Individual-Landmarks/ch5p-r223

This dataset is regularly updated and includes descriptive info.  Includes both designated and "calendared" (under consideration) individual landmarks.

The subset of points with lm_type = ‘Individual Landmark’ are commonly referred to as 'individual landmarks.'

# ArcGIS REST Services

As of winter 2020 the Landmarks Preservation Commission publishes ArcGIS REST data services supporting the [Discover New York City Landmarks](http://nyclpc.maps.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=93a88691cace4067828b1eede432022b) application, including Individual Landmarks at this ArcGIS online rest service.  It may be password-protected.
