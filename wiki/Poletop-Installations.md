# Open Data Portal

Mobile Telecommunication Franchise Poletop Installation Locations

https://data.cityofnewyork.us/City-Government/Mobile-Telecommunication-Franchise-Poletop-Install/tbgj-tdd6

This dataset is regularly updated and includes descriptive info.  As of August 2018 the data includes x/y coordinates in the New York Long Island State Plane (http://epsg.io/2263)  coordinate reference system.

"Mobile Telecommunications Franchises allow authorized companies to install and operate telecom equipment on street light poles, traffic light poles, and utility poles to facilitate wireless communications in the five boroughs. The franchises enable companies to use this equipment to enhance existing services or to provide new wireless services, including cellular and broadband Internet access."

From https://www1.nyc.gov/site/doitt/business/mobile-telecom-franchises.page
