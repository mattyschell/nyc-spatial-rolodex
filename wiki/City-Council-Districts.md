Under [Section 51](https://nyccharter.readthedocs.io/c02A/#section-51) of the New York City charter a commission draws New York city council districts after the decennial census.  The redistricting rules in the charter are ranked by importance and include restrictions on the number of people, geographic boundary length and width, and a fun puzzle to minimize the length of the sum of the boundaries.  The next round of new boundaries will be in place in time for the February 2023 City Council elections.
