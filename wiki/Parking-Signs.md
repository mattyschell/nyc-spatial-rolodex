The NYC Department of Transportation (DOT) publishes to NYC Open Data a dataset containing current and historical parking regulation signs.  The dataset is not spatial but does include a sign_x_coord and sign_y_coord column.

## NYC Open Data

Parking Regulation Locations and Signs: https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Transportation/Parking-Regulation-Locations-and-Signs/nfid-uabd/about_data
