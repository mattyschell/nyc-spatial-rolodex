The Department of Finance publishes subterranean lots on the [Property Information Portal](https://propertyinformationportal.nyc.gov/).  To support the Property Information Portal the Department of Finance publishes a subterranean lots service on the [NYCMaps](https://nyc.maps.arcgis.com/home/index.html) ArcGIS Online organization.  The Department of Finance also publishes this data in some form on the NYC Open Data Portal.

See [Tax-Map](./Tax-Map.md) for more background.
