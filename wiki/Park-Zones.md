As of summer 2018 The Department of Parks and Recreation releases this dataset monthly.  The Department of Information Technology and Telecommunications refreshes the open data portal with the monthly release from the Department of Parks and Recreation.

**Caution:** In the past the Parks Zones dataset on the Open Data Portal has been switcheroo'd with the Parks Properties dataset.  The Park Zones dataset should contain approximately 500 records.  Older links to the bad Park Zones dataset now redirect to Park Properties.
