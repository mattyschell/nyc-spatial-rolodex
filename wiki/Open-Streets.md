The NYC Department of Transportation started releasing Open Streets data in May 2020.  The team at the NYC Department of Transportation uses [LION](./Lion.md) from a 2018 vintage as the base for this dataset, so shapes and identifiers may not match current spatial data elsewhere in the city.

Open Streets and this corresponding data are in flux.  Expect frequent updates and changes to the schema. 

### Open Data Portal

As of early June 2020 this dataset includes [LION](./Lion.md) 2018 segment ids.  Use caution, LION segments can be duplicated so you will see vertical stacks of segments in some places, and adding up the lengths of all segments will sum up a length far greater than the City's stated Open Streets mileage.
