The New York City Department of Health and Mental Hygiene (DOHMH) maintains health center boundaries.  These are also known as "*Health Center* Service Areas." Health centers are used for reporting health statistics. [[Health-Areas]] nest within Health-Centers.

Older systems sometimes refer to these as Health Center Districts.
