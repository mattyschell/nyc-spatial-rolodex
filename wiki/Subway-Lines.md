### Open Data Portal

The NYC Department of Information Technology and Telecommunications Geographic Information Systems group maintains a subway lines dataset for use on cartographic products. It's not spatially accurate - editors have used cartographic license to manually separate and generalize lines.

https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Transportation/Subway-Lines/3qz8-muuu
