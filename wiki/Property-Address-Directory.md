The Property Address Directory (PAD) "is maintained by the NYC Department of City Planning, and includes every single address in New York City, including alternate street names, non-addressable places, vanity addresses, and more."

From https://medium.com/nyc-planning-digital/geowhat-a-quick-overview-of-nyc-geocoding-tools-b15655fd9207

It's the base data that feeds into all of New York City's geocoders.
