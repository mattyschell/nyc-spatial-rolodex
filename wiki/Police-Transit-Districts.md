Police Transit Districts are the responsibility of the Transit Bureau of the New York City Police Department (NYPD).

There doesn't appear to be any publicly released spatial data for Transit Districts (as of March 2019).  The links on this page can help to visualize Transit Districts:

https://www1.nyc.gov/site/nypd/bureaus/transit-housing/transit.page
