# Open Data Portal

https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Housing-Development/Scenic-Landmarks/gi7d-8gt5

This dataset is regularly updated and includes descriptive info.  Includes both designated and "calendared" (under consideration) scenic landmarks.


# ArcGIS REST Services

As of winter 2020 the Landmarks Preservation Commission publishes ArcGIS REST data services supporting the [Discover New York City Landmarks](http://nyclpc.maps.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=93a88691cace4067828b1eede432022b) application, including Scenic Landmarks at this (possibly password-protected) endpoint:

https://services5.arcgis.com/Oos4pNA2538iVFA1/ArcGIS/rest/services
